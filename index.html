Education in Egypt

Education is the basic thing in every one’s life. Everyone must acquire basic education to live in this society. Education is the sub system which performs some functions for the whole society. as we move from one generation to next generation we can face some changes in the education system Throughout the ages, education, especially in developing countries such as Egypt, has faced great problems, and officials have sought to solve these problems

Types of education

STEM education emphasizes learning applied sciences, engineering, mathematics, and technology. The students learn practical skills that will be useful for them in the future. These skills are in demand in today's world. STEM provides a hands-on experience to the students which is not available at traditional schools. In this type of school, students can get a degree or diploma that will help them land a job or go to university with ease.

Al-Azhar education is represented in the so-called Al-Azhar institutes, which are institutes belonging to Al-Azhar Al-Sharif in the Arab Republic of Egypt and contain all the different educational stages - it contains the secondary, preparatory and primary stages and includes boys and girls - in which the educational curriculum of the Ministry of Education is taught in addition to religious subjects such as monotheism, jurisprudence and hadith There are also higher universities in Al-Azhar from various scientific and practical colleges

Technical education in Egypt is one of the main tools for achieving comprehensive development programs, as it is the backbone of the economy and the locomotive of development. It includes about 220 technical specializations that serve all these fields. Therefore, technical education is entrusted with preparing the skilled workforce necessary to serve the plans and programs for the economic and social development of the state.

Learn how to learn

     Before improving education in Egypt you must Learn how to learn? First we must know what learning means Learning is divided into two categories: focused mode & diffused mode.
Focused mode: occurs when focusing on certain things such as work, integration with the computer, study, and integration with books
Diffused mode: It occurs when resting and not thinking and releasing ideas linked to each other in the brain and usually good ideas and this situation is repeated before bedtime where the brain connects thoughts to each other.
II. Memory is divided into two categories: short-term and long-term
Short-term: stores new information but does not maintain it for a long time
Long term: A large and spacious place that stores information for long periods
