Since 1950, we have faced multiple crises on multiple continents, and provided vital protection and assistance to refugees, asylum-seekers, internally displaced and stateless people, many of whom have nobody left to turn to. We help to save lives and build better futures for millions forced from home.

Types of refugees

Refugees fleeing wars and armed conflicts: These refugees flee their countries due to violent conflicts and armed wars sweeping their countries, and they are considered one of the most common types of refugees.

Political refugees: These refugees flee their countries because of their political persecution, and this is because they belong to different political groups or because they are subjected to political persecution.

Economic refugees: These refugees flee their countries in search of work and better opportunities in other countries, and are often looking for better opportunities to improve their standard of living.

Learn how to help refugees

It helps refugees in many different ways, the most important of which are:

  • Volunteering in humanitarian organizations: You can contact the various organizations working to help refugees, and volunteer with them to provide support and assistance.
  • Donations: You can donate money or food, medicine, clothes, blankets, sheets, furniture, and other essential items that refugees need.
  • Hosting: You can host refugees in your home or secure housing for them, providing them with food, clothing, medicine and emotional support.
  • Fundraising campaigns: You can work on launching campaigns to collect financial donations, food, medicine, clothes, furniture and blankets, which can help provide a decent life for refugees.
